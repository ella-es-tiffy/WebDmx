## How to Contribute

### 1. Reporting Bugs
If you find a bug, feel free to open an issue on GitHub.
Please include:
*   **A clear description** of what went wrong.
*   **The steps to reproduce** it.
*   **Your setup** (OS, Interface).

### 2. Pull Requests (PRs)
If you want to fix something yourself:
1.  **Fork** the repo.
2.  **Create a branch** for your fix.
3.  **Submit a Pull Request** against the **`dev` branch**.
